As your Momentum Coach, I'm here to help you move from feeling stuck, to taking purposeful steps forward and embracing new possibilities with confidence and excitement. I’m all about helping you overcome inertia, find clarity, and build momentum toward your goals. I help you tap into your inner drive, wisdom, and enthusiasm to set and achieve meaningful intentions. Whether you want to reinvent your career, improve your personal life, or start a new adventure, I'll guide you in creating a clear, actionable roadmap that leads to a compelling future, while making you feel truly seen, heard, and valued. I’m here to help you break free from uncertainty, set meaningful intentions, and reach your highest potential, so you can feel authentically alive! Let’s connect and get inspired together.

Workshops

At Crooked Arrow Coaching, I offer workshops that blend creative expression and deep personal introspection to empower you on your journey of becoming authentically alive.

  • CREATIVITY WORKSHOP: Explore your imagination to help you design your future. Crooked Arrow has workshops in art, writing, and various forms of self-expression, unlocking your creativity to help you dream bigger and bolder.

  • AFFIRMATION CARDS WORKSHOP: Design your own personalized affirmation cards to empower your life with positivity and intention.

  • CORE VALUES WORKSHOP: Delve into core values exploration to discern what truly drives you, aligning your life choices with your authentic values for a more purposeful existence.

  • WRITE YOUR MANIFESTO: Discover your life's purpose through an immersive workshop designed to clarify your passions and guide future decisions with a personalized Life Purpose Statement and Manifesto.
